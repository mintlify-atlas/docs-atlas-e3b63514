The Keiyoushi catalogue contains over 1,352 extensions, but a handful stand out as the most widely installed starting points. This page highlights notable sources across four categories — official publisher platforms, large multi-language aggregators, English scanlation hubs, and art and illustration platforms. For each source you will find the APK filename to identify it in the repository, what kind of content it serves, and any important caveats such as account requirements or adult content.

<Note>
  This is a selection of notable sources. Browse the full catalogue at [https://keiyoushi.github.io/extensions/](https://keiyoushi.github.io/extensions/).
</Note>

## Official publisher sources

These extensions connect to platforms run by publishers or rights holders and serve officially licensed content. They are the most reliable sources for current simulpublication chapters.

| Source | APK | Languages | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| MANGA Plus by SHUEISHA | `tachiyomi-all.mangaplus-v1.4.60.apk` | en, es, fr, id, pt-BR, ru, th, vi, de | Free with account required; first and latest chapters only for most titles |
| MANGA Plus Creators by SHUEISHA | `tachiyomi-all.mangapluscreators-v1.4.2.apk` | en, es | Creator-upload platform; mix of free and paid chapters |
| VIZ | `tachiyomi-en.vizshonenjump-v1.4.22.apk` | en | Exposes both VIZ Shonen Jump and VIZ Manga sources; subscription required for full access |
| Tapas | `tachiyomi-en.tapastic-v1.4.24.apk` | en | Webcomic and webtoon platform; mix of free and ink-gated (paid) episodes; **NSFW** content present |
| Webtoons.com | `tachiyomi-all.webtoons-v1.4.52.apk` | en, id, th, es, fr, zh-Hant, de | Official LINE Webtoons platform; free with optional fast-pass purchases |
| BookWalker Global | `tachiyomi-en.bookwalker-v1.4.5.apk` | en | Digital manga storefront; browsing is free but reading requires purchase; **NSFW** flag set |

<Warning>
  Official publisher sources are subject to their own terms of service. Using an extension to access content does not override regional availability restrictions, chapter limits, or account requirements imposed by the publisher. Check each platform's terms before use.
</Warning>

## Large multi-language aggregators

These extensions cover content in many languages and aggregate titles from numerous original sources. They are typically the broadest starting point for readers who want a single extension covering a wide catalogue.

| Source | APK | Languages | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| MangaDex | `tachiyomi-all.mangadex-v1.4.207.apk` | 40+ languages | Community scanlation database; requires a free account to track reading history and access some settings; **NSFW** flag set (adult content can be enabled in settings) |
| MangaFire | `tachiyomi-all.mangafire-v1.4.20.apk` | en, es, es-419, fr, ja, pt, pt-BR | Aggregator with both manga and anime content sections; **NSFW** flag set |
| Manganato | `tachiyomi-en.manganelo-v1.4.15.apk` | en | Large English-language aggregator; **NSFW** flag set |
| MangaTaro | `tachiyomi-all.mangataro-v1.4.11.apk` | en, pt-BR | Multi-language aggregator with broad genre coverage |

<Note>
  MangaDex exposes one source entry per language in your app's **Browse → Sources** list. After installing the extension, you will see a large number of MangaDex sources — one for English, one for Japanese, and so on. You can pin the language sources you want to use.
</Note>

## English scanlation hubs

These extensions connect to English-language scanlation and reading sites that aggregate fan translations or host independently produced scans. Most are free to use but may carry NSFW-flagged content.

| Source | APK | NSFW | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Asura Scans | `tachiyomi-en.asurascans-v1.4.60.apk` | No | Popular scanlation site with a large catalogue of action and fantasy manhwa |
| Hive Scans | `tachiyomi-en.infernalvoidscans-v1.4.62.apk` | No | Scanlation group site; package name reflects the group's previous name (Infernal Void Scans) |
| Dynasty | `tachiyomi-en.dynasty-v1.4.29.apk` | Yes | Dynasty Scans — specialises in yuri and girls-love manga; source name in app is **Dynasty Scans** |
| Toonily | `tachiyomi-en.toonily-v1.4.61.apk` | Yes | Large manhwa reading site with a significant adult content section |
| Hiperdex | `tachiyomi-en.hiperdex-v1.4.75.apk` | Yes | Aggregator covering manga, manhwa, and manhua; adult content section present |
| MangaBuddy | `tachiyomi-en.mangabuddy-v1.4.22.apk` | Yes | General manga reading site with a broad catalogue; adult content present |

<Warning>
  Community scanlation sites operate independently of rights holders. Content availability may change without notice, and sites may go offline or change their domain. The Keiyoushi community updates extensions when known breaking changes occur, but there is no guarantee of continuity.
</Warning>

## Art and illustration platforms

| Source | APK | Languages | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pixiv | `tachiyomi-all.pixiv-v1.4.12.apk` | en, ja, zh, zh-tw, ko | Japanese illustration and manga platform; free account required; **NSFW** flag set — adult content is common and controlled by the source's own settings |

Pixiv surfaces manga series (`pixiv_manga_series`) and individual illustration sets uploaded by creators on [pixiv.net](https://www.pixiv.net). Because Pixiv is a creator platform rather than a curated reading site, the extension gives you access to a very large volume of user-uploaded content rather than a structured catalogue. Expect varied quality and mixed languages within a single search.
