Keiyoushi Extensions is the community-maintained extension repository for [Mihon](https://mihon.app) and all major Tachiyomi forks. It provides over 1,352 source extensions — packaged as Android APKs — that let your reader app connect to manga, manhwa, manhua, and webcomic sites across more than 20 languages.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your app's extension repository settings">
    In Mihon or any Tachiyomi fork, go to **Browse → Extension repositories** (or **Settings → Browse**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Keiyoushi repository URL">
    Enter the following URL and tap **Add**:
    ```
    https://raw.githubusercontent.com/keiyoushi/extensions/repo/index.min.json
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install extensions you want">
    Go to **Browse → Extensions**, find the sources you want, and tap **Install**. You can filter by language or search by name.
  </Step>
</Steps>
